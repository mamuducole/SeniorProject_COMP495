Scholarship Program

Investing in Future Leaders

THE ROCKS, INC. Scholarship Program recognizes students who demonstrate academic excellence, leadership potential, and a meaningful commitment to service. Through scholarship support, we help empower the next generation of leaders as they pursue higher education and future impact.

Program Overview

Our scholarship initiative highlights and supports students who are committed to achievement in the classroom, growth in leadership, and service within their schools and communities.

By recognizing scholars from ROTC and university communities, THE ROCKS, INC. continues its mission of strengthening leadership pipelines and celebrating students who reflect excellence, discipline, and purpose.

Academic Achievement

Scholarship recipients reflect dedication to higher education and a strong commitment to personal and professional growth.

Leadership Potential

Applicants are recognized not just for grades, but for leadership, initiative, campus involvement, and the example they set for others.

Service & Impact

THE ROCKS, INC. values students who use their talents to serve, uplift their communities, and prepare for lives of meaningful contribution.

Scholarship Focus

Our scholarships highlight students who are preparing to lead with character, confidence, and a commitment to excellence. Recipients reflect the mission of THE ROCKS, INC. through academic success, leadership engagement, and service-minded ambition.

Why It Matters

Financial support can reduce barriers, strengthen opportunity, and help students remain focused on their educational journey. Every scholarship awarded is an investment in future leadership.

Additional Scholarship Opportunity

Alfred Street Baptist Church (ASBC) Foundation Scholarships

The ASBC Foundation annually awards scholarships ranging from $1,000 to $30,000. Most awards are one-time scholarships, while several are renewable for up to 4 years, with renewable awards ranging from $20,000 to $30,000.

Students who complete the application and meet the general eligibility requirements are considered for all scholarships, with some awards including additional specific guidelines and requirements.
