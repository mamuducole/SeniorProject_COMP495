Mentorship Program

Building Leaders Through Mentorship

The ROCKS Mentorship Program is a comprehensive initiative that supports career growth, strengthens leadership, and fosters a professional community within the Army and Department of Defense.

Program Overview

The ROCKS Mentorship Program provides structured guidance, professional development, and networking opportunities for ROTC cadets, military officers, and Department of Defense civilians.

The ROCKS, Inc. is a non-profit organization dedicated to strengthening the U.S. Army officer corps through mentorship, leadership development, scholarships, and networking opportunities. The mentorship program connects junior officers, warrant officers, and DoD civilians with experienced mentors who provide guidance on career development, professional skills, and personal growth.

Who Can Participate

Eligible participants include ROTC and Military Academy cadets, active duty or reserve component commissioned officers, active duty or reserve component warrant officers, and Department of Defense civilians at the GS-12 level and above.

Structure & Activities

The program is carried out through chapters and interest groups across the country. Mentorship is typically one-on-one and may include leadership outreach initiatives, seminars, career guidance, and professional development support.

Benefits

Participants receive personalized career guidance, professional development, networking with senior officers and experienced DoD civilians, and stronger readiness for the transition from college to active military service.

Leadership and Oversight

The program is overseen by the Vice President for Mentorship at the national level, who ensures adherence to policy and supports effective mentoring across chapters. Local chapter mentorship chairs coordinate activities, match mentors with mentees, and help provide ongoing support.

How to Join

Interested individuals can join by contacting their local ROCKS chapter or by submitting information through chapter-specific mentorship forms. Questions are typically directed to the local chapter mentorship chair for guidance on next steps.

Get Involved

Whether you are looking to grow as a leader or give back through mentorship, THE ROCKS Mentorship Program provides opportunities to connect, develop, and lead. The program supports ROTC cadets, military officers, and Department of Defense civilians through structured mentorship, professional development, and meaningful relationships.

By joining, you become part of a network committed to strengthening leadership, fostering excellence, and making a lasting impact through service.
